PT. Green Chemicals Indonesia (GREENCHEM)

PT Green Chemicals Indonesia (Greenchem) is a chemical manufacturing company and we are dedicated to produce the best chemical product through research, development, and innovation.

The revamp focused on functionality, access swiftly, and user friendly experience for new visitor. We optimized the structure of the website and incorporated advanced search features to improve its functionality. By following web accessibility guidelines, we guaranteed that the platform could be used by everyone. Enhancements to the user experience were achieved through a design approach that prioritizes the user, intuitive navigation, and simplified product categorization. We designed the visual components to connect with the intended audience, embracing a simplistic style that emphasized the product as the primary focal point.The design strategy was geared towards establishing an interactive and user-friendly platform.
